Hi there, USERNAME!

We know that you want to start stacking immediately, but first we need to explain a few things. It won't take long, we promise ;)

Feed
When you log in, you see your personal feed. The feed shows you pictures that other users uploaded to the stack. For every photo in the stack you have a few options:

  • If you like the picture, click the like button or use the right arrow on your keyboard.
  • If you don’t like the picture, click the dislike button or use the left arrow on your keyboard.
  • If you think that the picture is inappropriate, click Inappropriate

If you want to follow the user that uploaded the photo, click follow. After you’ve added some friends by clicking on the follow button. You can choose to go to the friendfeed. This feed will only show you the new pictures of the users that you marked as friend.

Upload
Before you can upload something to the stack, you need to determine if you want to upload a gif from the Giphy database or a picture from your own device.

  • If you want to use a picture from your own device, click the button at Upload a picture to the stack
  • If you want to search the Giphy database for a gif, enter a keyword in the gif keyword field and click Gimme giphy’s.

Next enter a description to your picture and click submit (or if you've selected a gif from the Giphy database, click the gif). You’ve added something to the stack!

Now that you know the most important functions of LikeStack, have fun stacking!
